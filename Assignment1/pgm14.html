Fruits

Apple
An apple is a sweet, edible fruit produced by an apple tree. Apples are rich in fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants.

Banana
A banana is a long, curved fruit with a yellow peel. Bananas are an excellent source of potassium and other vitamins.

Orange
An orange is a citrus fruit known for its tangy flavor. It is an excellent source of vitamin C and other nutrients.

Strawberry
A strawberry is a small, red fruit with tiny seeds on the surface. It is rich in antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals.

Grapes
Grapes are small, round, and typically purple or green. They are high in vitamins and antioxidants, and they can be eaten fresh or dried as raisins.
